Company Overview

ZimRe Holdings Limited “ZHL” is a diversified investment holding company with sustainable core competencies in insurance and property businesses. It has investments and operations located in Zimbabwe and the Southern African Region. ZimRe Holdings Limited is an active growth-oriented investor that provides strategic leadership and guidance and ensures value creation and portfolio optimization.


History

  • ZimRe was established in 1983 as a public corporation through an Act of Parliament and started transacting reinsurance business in 1984.

  • In 1985 ZimRe set up Zimbabwe Insurance Brokers as a Greenfield investment

  • In 1988, ZimRe acquired Legal and General (now Fidelity Life) and NEM Insurance (Nicoz)

  • ZimRe Malawi was established in 1996 after acquiring a controlling stake in Pearl Assurance Malawi (UGI)

  • ZimRe was privatized by the Zimbabwe Government and subsequently listed on the Zimbabwe Stock Exchange on 22 November 1999, thus broadening the shareholder base.

  • By 1999, when the Company was privatized, it was operating in the Reinsurance, General Insurance, Life and Pensions, Insurance Broking sectors and had other investments within Zimbabwe, Malawi, Zambia and South Africa.

  • In 2002, shareholders approved the unbundling of the Group into strategic business units, which were highly focused, and sector specific in order to increase shareholder value

  • The unbundling process started with the listing of NicozDiamond in October 2002

  • The demerger of the reinsurance operations from Head Office was implemented in January 2003.

  • Fidelity Life Assurance was listed in June 2003.

  • ZPI listed in July 2007
